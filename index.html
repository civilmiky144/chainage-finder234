How Chainage Works
This app has 4,889 chainage points from your KML file already built in (0+000 to 244+354, every 50 m).

When you start tracking, it finds the nearest chainage point to your GPS location and displays its name. No calculation — just direct lookup. Very accurate and reliable.
Using the App
1
Tap ▶ Start Tracking and allow location permission
2
Wait outdoors for the GPS dot to turn green (good accuracy)
3
Your nearest chainage updates live as you move
4
Tap 📍 Mark Point Here to save location with a note
GPS Accuracy
🍺 Green = <10 m accuracy (excellent)
🉑 Yellow = <25 m (acceptable)
🔴 Red = >25 m (poor — wait for better fix)

Nearest Pt Dist shows how far you are from the closest chainage point. If this is over 50 m, you may be between two points.
